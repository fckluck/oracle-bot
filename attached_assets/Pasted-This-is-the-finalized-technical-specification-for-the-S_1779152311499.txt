# **Technical Specification: Sure Shot CTO System v2.1**

### **1. Entry Grade & Position Sizing Logic**
The bot must evaluate the **Volume-to-Liquidity (Vol/Liq) Ratio** as the primary conviction trigger.
*   **BASELINE ENTRY (5x - 7.9x Vol/Liq):** Recommend **1x Standard Unit**.
*   **HIGH CONVICTION (8x - 11.9x Vol/Liq):** Recommend **1.5x Standard Unit**.
*   **GOD TIER (12x+ Vol/Liq):** Recommend **2x Standard Unit**.

### **2. Dynamic Holder Gate (Market Cap Scaled)**
Instead of a static holder count, use a dynamic baseline of **400 holders per $100K Market Cap**.
*   **Calculation:** `(Current MC / 100,000) * 400` = Target Baseline.
*   **The Safety Range:**
    *   **Floor (50% of Baseline):** If holders are below this, flag `LOW ORGANIC REACH`.
    *   **Ceiling (200% of Baseline):** If holders are above this, flag `INFLATED WALLETS`.
*   **Exception:** If holder data is unavailable, flag as `UNVERIFIED` (Do not auto-skip; let the human decide).

### **3. Transaction Pressure Context**
Display these flags based on the ratio of Buys vs. Sells in the last 5-15 minutes:
*   **AGGRESSIVE BUY PRESSURE:** If Buys are **> 85%** of total transactions.
*   **SELL PRESSURE WARNING:** If Buys are **< 40%** of total transactions.

### **4. Mandatory Hard Filters**
The bot must **SKIP** or **AUTO-FAIL** any token meeting these conditions:
*   **Serial Deployer:** Developer has launched **> 10 tokens**.
*   **Low Liquidity:** Total LP is **< $10,000**.
*   **CTO Status:** No verified Community Takeover (CTO) flag detected via DexScreener/Socials.

### **5. The "Dead Zone" Filter (Time-Based)**
Between **12 PM – 7 PM (Local/Standard Time)**:
*   Scale all Market Cap targets down by **30%**.
*   Require a minimum of **8x Vol/Liq** for any "Entry" signal.

### **6. Automated Exit Strategy**
Once a trade is live, the bot must monitor for the following "Trim" and "Exit" signals:
*   **The 75% Trim (Sell 75%):** Trigger if **Vol/Liq falls below 2x** OR **Holder growth is < 1% over 10m**.
*   **The Moon Bag (Hold 25%):**
    *   Set a Hard Stop Loss at **50% Market Cap retracement** from the local All-Time High (ATH).
